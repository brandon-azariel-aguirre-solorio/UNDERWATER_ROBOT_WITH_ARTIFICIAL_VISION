Artificial vision, an artificial vision model is developed trained to recognize 15 types of marine species corresponding to the coastal area of ​​Manzanillo: crocodiles, octopuses, whales, sharks, dolphins, crabs, turtles, fish (by small, medium and large sizes), sea urchins, manta rays, eels, orcas, seahorses, starfish and jellyfish.
